His Life Story

John Kayode Adebola was born on 16 August 1972 in Nigeria, beginning a life journey that would span continents and touch countless hearts. Growing up in Kano, he attended St Louis Primary School and had just started at St Thomas Secondary School when his life took an exciting turn.

His move to Hull, England - his mother Margaret's hometown - marked the beginning of a new chapter. There, John continued his education and embraced the opportunities that lay ahead while keeping his Nigerian heritage close to his heart.

John's academic journey led him to Essex University, where he pursued Computer Science with dedication and passion. After completing his degree, he settled in Hampstead, London, where he would build not just a home, but a legacy.

As an entrepreneur, John established successful mobile phone businesses in Streatham and Camden Town. His shops became more than places of business - they were community hubs where his warmth, expertise, and genuine care for others shone through every interaction.
